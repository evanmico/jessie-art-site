About Me

Hello! You stopped by here? Why? Eh, I guess you must be interested in learning more about myself as an artist. Well alright, you win, I'll share. Art has been my passion since a very early age; however, now that I have started university (biomedical engineering major) I have had to put that on the back burner. Some of my early works were actually art of many SEGA games, particularly the classic Sonic. When I was 7, I drew some of my earliest renditions of the character and have been drawing ever since. Of course, nowadays my art has become much better. A lot of my art features the color purple and the reason for that is that it is my favorite color. It is also why this website is primary shades of purple :)
